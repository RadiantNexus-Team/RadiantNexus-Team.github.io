Sonic 3 Limitless



A RadiantNexus Project



Sonic 3 Limitless is a RadiantNexus ROM hack project which seeks to add quality of life changes from 3AIR and Origins to hardware, while also porting fanmade arrangemnets to hardware as well.

This smaller project aims to include:



- Complete Options menu

- Entirely revamped art.

- Remixed music from a variaty of composers

- Revamped Tails spritesheet

- Tweaked spritesheets

- Quality of Life changes to enhance the player experience.

- Bug Fixes

- Extra polish

- For any tech wizards who care: Sonic and Knuckles half being backported to Sonic 3 for the full game in one.
